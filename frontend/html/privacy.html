Privacy Policy

How TaskFlow collects, protects, and uses information.

This Privacy Policy explains how TaskFlow handles information when customers, workspace members, and visitors use our website and platform. It is designed to give teams a clear view of our data practices.

Information Collection

We collect information you provide directly, such as account details, workspace configuration data, task content, uploaded files, support requests, and communication preferences.

Account Data

Account records may include names, email addresses, role assignments, login activity, and workspace participation details needed to authenticate users and operate the service.

Cookies

TaskFlow uses cookies and similar technologies to maintain sessions, remember preferences such as language selection, and improve website functionality and usability.

Security

We use technical and operational safeguards intended to protect data from unauthorized access, misuse, disclosure, or loss, including access controls and monitored authentication flows.

Data Usage

Information is used to provide the platform, maintain workspace functionality, support customers, improve performance, analyze usage patterns, and communicate important product or account updates.

Third-party Services

We may use trusted third-party service providers for authentication, hosting, analytics, or communications. These providers are engaged only to support platform delivery and related operations.

User Rights

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of eligible personal data, subject to applicable law, contractual obligations, and legitimate operational or security requirements.

Data Retention

We retain data for as long as needed to provide services, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and preserve reasonable business records and security logs.

Contact Information

Questions about privacy practices can be sent to privacy@taskflowapp.com or mailed to TaskFlow, Indiranagar, Bengaluru, Karnataka, India.
